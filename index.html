About Us

Since our establishment in 1965, Hindustan Engineering Works has been a trusted leader in high-quality fabrication and metalwork across the NCR, specifically serving the Ghaziabad and Noida regions. With over six decades of expertise and more than 250+ successfully completed contracts, we take pride in our proven track record of delivering excellence to a diverse range of satisfied clients.

Our prestigious portfolio includes major projects for landmarks such as the Country Inn hotel in Ghaziabad and GIP Mall in Noida, reflecting our ability to meet the highest industry standards. Led by Mohd. Idris-Azam Khan, we specialize in the craftsmanship of stainless steel, aluminum, and wrought iron gates, as well as grills, metro shades, and advanced motorized shutters. Driven by a vision to expand our reach by 70%, we remain committed to providing durable, aesthetic, and precision-engineered solutions for every customer.
